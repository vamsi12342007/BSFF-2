Synopsis

The Last Note is a poignant tale of a father's unwavering love and sacrifice.This emotional short film is about the journey of a father who goes to extraordinary lengths to support his son's dream of becoming a musician.This is a heartfelt tribute to every parent who sacrifices themselves to build a future for their children.
